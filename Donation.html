Donate For Humanity

Donate via bKash

To donate via bKash, follow these steps:

  1. Open your bKash app
  2. Select "Send Money"
  3. Enter the recipient's bKash number: 01XXXXXXXXX
  4. Enter the donation amount
  5. Enter a reference (optional)
  6. Confirm the transaction
